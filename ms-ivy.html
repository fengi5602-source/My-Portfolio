Analytical Frameworks: Why Study Disparity?

The Economic Imperative: Welfare & Inequality

Modern economies face critical challenges in wealth distribution and access to welfare resources. My work in econometrics and political economy seeks to quantify and understand the structural causes of these disparities. The goal is to move beyond correlation to establish causal links for effective policy interventions.

Explore Economic Analysis

Machine Learning for Exacerbating Wealth Gaps

While ML offers powerful solutions, biased data or poorly designed algorithms can unintentionally exacerbate wealth inequalities, particularly in areas like credit scoring, resource allocation, and job market filtering. My ML projects emphasize explainability (XAI) and fairness to ensure technological potential serves equitable outcomes.
